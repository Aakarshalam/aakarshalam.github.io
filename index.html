FPGA Architecture · Published

FlashAttention Design on FPGA

IISc Bangalore · Prof. Sumit K Mandal

May 2025 – Present ▼
  • Devised a 3-stage FlashAttention architecture for Zedboard to accelerate Transformer self-attention for throughput and latency.
  • Implemented online softmax with negative exponential engine, achieving error <0.05% for 32-bit, optimised for parallel operations.
  • Verified the PS-PL flow for Stage 2 by running a host application on ARM (Zynq PS), achieving latency <50 ns per tile on average.
  • Integrated all 3 stages for tile sizes 4, 8, & 16; achieved a 2.9× decrement in latency over SOTA for tile size 64.
  • Work published as a poster at ISFPGA 2026 (ACM/SIGDA), pp. 179–179.
